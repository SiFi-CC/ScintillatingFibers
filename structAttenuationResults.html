Detailed Description

Class to determine attenuation length. This class is suitable only for experimental series with different positions of source. Two methods of attenuation length determination are available: AttAveragedCh() - based on Pauwels et al., JINST 8 (2013) P09019, where combined signal from both channels is analyzed, and AttSeparateCh()

  • where attenuation length is calulated for each channel separately.
